About Me

little information

Who I AM?

I am a passionate student pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, driven by a deep fascination for the intersection of technology and design. With a keen eye for aesthetics and a knack for problem-solving, I aspire to become a UI/UX designer who creates engaging and user-centric digital experiences.

What I Do?

As a budding UI/UX designer, I specialize in translating complex ideas into intuitive, visually appealing designs. Proficient in tools like Adobe XD, Figma, and Sketch, I create wireframes, prototypes, and interfaces prioritizing user experience and functionality.

Why I Do It?

I'm driven by a passion for crafting impactful experiences that enhance user lives. Great design simplifies complexities, evokes emotions, and leaves lasting impressions. By leveraging technical skills and creative insights, I aim to bridge technology and human interaction, enriching how people engage with digital products.
